# Pricing Policy

## Standard Pricing Tiers

### Starter Plan
- **Monthly Cost**: $99
- **Seats**: Up to 5 users
- **Email Volume**: Up to 1,000 emails/month
- **Storage**: 10 GB
- **Features**: Basic email management, thread linking, priority scoring
- **Support**: Community forum

### Professional Plan
- **Monthly Cost**: $499
- **Seats**: Up to 25 users
- **Email Volume**: Up to 10,000 emails/month
- **Storage**: 100 GB
- **Features**: All Starter features + RAG knowledge retrieval, AI classification, custom workflows
- **Support**: Email support (24-hour response)
- **SLA**: 99.5% uptime guarantee

### Enterprise Plan
- **Custom Pricing**: Starting at $2,000/month
- **Seats**: Unlimited
- **Email Volume**: Unlimited
- **Storage**: Unlimited
- **Features**: All Professional features + API access, custom integrations, dedicated account manager
- **Support**: 24/7 phone support, dedicated Slack channel
- **SLA**: 99.95% uptime guarantee

## Volume Discounts

- 10-50% discount for annual prepayment
- 15-20% discount for multi-year agreements (3+ years)
- Additional 10% discount for nonprofits and educational institutions
- Reseller pricing available for partners

## Pro-Rata Billing

When customers add or remove users mid-cycle:
- Daily rate: Plan price / number of calendar days in month
- Billed at upgrade/downgrade effective date
- Pro-rata credits applied to next billing cycle if refund-eligible

## Nonprofit Discount Program

- Eligible nonprofits receive 50% discount on all plans
- Requires 501(c)(3) certification or equivalent
- Apply via nonprofit@company.com with supporting documentation
- Annual prepayment required for discount

## Billing Cycles

- Monthly billing: Charged on the same date each month
- Annual billing: 2 months free (effective 16.67% discount)
- Payment methods: Credit card, ACH transfer, invoice (Enterprise only)
- Billing address must be verified before first charge

## Add-ons

- **Premium API Access**: $299/month (rate limit increase to 10,000 req/min)
- **Custom Branding**: $199/month
- **Advanced Analytics**: $149/month
- **White-label Solution**: Contact sales

## Trial Period

- 14-day free trial available for all plans
- No credit card required
- Full feature access during trial
- Trial converted to paid plan at end unless cancelled
