# Refund Policy

## Refund Windows

### 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

- Available for all plans during first 14 calendar days
- Applies only to initial payment
- Full refund if customer notifies within window
- Renewal fees not eligible for money-back guarantee

### Pro-Rata Refunds for Mid-Cycle Downgrades

- Customer may downgrade to lower plan at any time
- Pro-rata credit calculated from downgrade date to next billing date
- Credit applied as account balance, not cash refund
- Exception: Downgrade within 5 days of upgrade incurs restocking fee (15% of upgrade amount)

## Credits vs Refunds

### Automatic Credits

- SLA violations: Automatic credits applied per SLA terms
- Service disruptions >1 hour: 5% credit automatically applied
- Failed billing attempts: No charge, automatic retry after 5 days

### Refund Eligibility

Credits converted to refund only if:

1. Customer initiates cancellation
2. Account has positive balance from credits
3. Final invoice issued
4. Refund processed within 30 business days

## Retention Offers

For customers requesting cancellation:

### Eligible Customers (Enterprise)
- Usage >50% of plan allocation
- Account age >6 months
- Payment history clean

### Retention Options
- 1-month free service (for annual commit)
- Downgrade to lower plan (pro-rata credit applied)
- Pause subscription for up to 60 days
- 20-30% discount for next 6 months

### Decision Timeline
- Retention decision made within 24 hours of cancellation request
- Offer expires in 3 days if not accepted
- Pause option can be extended once per year

## Churn Handling

### Win-Back Program

Customers who cancelled are eligible for:

- 30% discount on any plan for first 3 months
- No setup fees
- Data preservation for up to 1 year
- Historical data reactivation available

### Cancellation Process

1. Customer initiates cancellation via account settings
2. System triggers retention workflow (24-hour hold)
3. If no response, cancellation proceeds
4. Final export of customer data available for 30 days
5. Hard delete after 90 days (GDPR compliance)

## Special Circumstances

### Business Failure / Hardship
- Customers facing financial hardship may request hardship refund
- Requires documentation of hardship
- Up to 1 refund per customer per 12 months
- Limited to 50% of fees paid in preceding 30 days

### Service Failure Refunds
- If service unavailable >72 hours per month: 100% refund for that month
- If service unavailable >168 hours per quarter: 100% refund for that quarter
- Refunds issued within 15 business days of request

### Tax Adjustments
- Refunds for tax over-collection (invoice corrections)
- Customer must provide corrected tax documentation
- Processed within 10 business days

## Refund Payments

- Original payment method: 5-10 business days
- Bank transfer: 3-5 business days
- Check (Enterprise): 10-15 business days
- Credit card issuer delays outside our control

## Policy Changes

- Refund policy changes apply only to new subscriptions
- Existing customers grandfathered under current policy for 12 months
- Major reductions in refund eligibility require 90-day notice
