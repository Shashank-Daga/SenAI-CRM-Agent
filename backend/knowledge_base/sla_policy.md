# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Uptime Guarantee

### Service Availability

- **Professional Plan**: 99.5% monthly uptime
- **Enterprise Plan**: 99.95% monthly uptime
- Calculated as: (Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month
- Scheduled maintenance excluded from SLA calculation

### Measurement

- Uptime measured from multiple geographic locations
- Real-time status dashboard available at status.company.com
- Monthly SLA report generated by 5th of following month

## Incident Response Times

### Professional Plan

| Severity | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Critical | 1 hour | 4 hours |
| High | 4 hours | 8 hours |
| Medium | 1 business day | 3 business days |
| Low | 2 business days | 5 business days |

### Enterprise Plan

| Severity | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Critical | 15 minutes | 2 hours |
| High | 30 minutes | 4 hours |
| Medium | 2 hours | 1 business day |
| Low | 1 business day | 2 business days |

## SLA Credits

If Company fails to meet availability targets:

### Professional Plan
- 99.0% - 99.49% uptime: 10% monthly fee credit
- 95.0% - 98.99% uptime: 25% monthly fee credit
- Below 95.0% uptime: 100% monthly fee credit

### Enterprise Plan
- 99.0% - 99.94% uptime: 10% monthly fee credit
- 95.0% - 98.99% uptime: 25% monthly fee credit
- Below 95.0% uptime: 100% monthly fee credit

### Credit Claim Process
1. Customer submits request within 30 days of incident
2. Provide evidence of outage and business impact
3. Credits applied to next month's invoice
4. Cumulative credit per month capped at 100% of monthly fee

## Incident Severity Definitions

### Critical
- Complete service outage
- Database unavailable
- Core API endpoints returning errors
- Affects >25% of customer base

### High
- Partial functionality degradation
- Response times >5 seconds for normal operations
- Affects 5-25% of customer base

### Medium
- Non-critical features unavailable
- Affects <5% of customer base
- Workaround available

### Low
- Cosmetic issues
- Minor functionality impact
- No customer data loss

## Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

- All Critical incidents require RCA within 5 business days
- RCA shared with affected customer via secure portal
- Customer may request RCA meeting within Enterprise support

## Exclusions

SLA does not cover:

- Customer configuration errors
- Third-party service failures (AWS, SendGrid, etc.)
- Denial of service attacks
- Beta or trial services
- Issues caused by customer's network or systems

## Support Escalation

1. **Level 1**: Standard support team (24-hour response)
2. **Level 2**: Senior engineer (4-hour response)
3. **Level 3**: Director of Engineering (2-hour response, Enterprise only)
